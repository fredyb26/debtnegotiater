Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Debt Negotiator?

It’s a simple way to ask for help if you’re behind on payments. Pick a plan, send it in, and we’ll review it.

Who can use this?

It’s for customers 90+ days past due who haven’t used it before. It’s a one-time exception to help you get back on track.

Can I do this more than once?

No, this is a one-time request.

Will this affect my credit?

Submitting won’t hurt your credit, but we report all activity accurately to the credit bureaus. Sticking to your plan can help improve your standing.

How long until I hear back?

We’ll get back to you in 1–2 business days.

What if none of the plans work for me?

You can suggest your own plan and we’ll take a look.

What if I need more help later?

This page is a one-time tool, but you can always call us to talk through other options.

What happens if my autopay doesn’t go through?

Call us right away. If no payment is made, your agreement could be voided and your account may go back to delinquent status.

When will I see the discount?

The discount will be applied within 5 business days after we receive your final payment.
