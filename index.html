Frequently Asked Questions

Will this affect my credit?

Filling out the form won’t impact your credit. It’s just a request. But once a plan is approved, we’re required to report your account accurately and completely to the credit bureaus. Sticking to the plan can help you get back on track — and this is a great first step.

How long does it take to hear back?

Our team usually reviews requests within 1–2 business days. We’ll reach out by phone or email once we’ve had a chance to look everything over.

What happens if my autopay payments don’t go through?

If a scheduled autopay fails, we strongly urge you to call us right away to make a payment. If no payment is received, we may consider the agreement void. Staying in touch is the best way to keep your plan active.

Can I do this more than once?

No, this program is limited to one request per customer. Once you submit a plan, you won’t be able to submit another through this page.

What if none of the payment plans work for me?

You can suggest your own plan using the “custom offer” option on the form. We’ll take a look and let you know if we can make it work.

When will I see the discount applied to my account?

The discount will be applied within 5 business days after we receive your final payment. Make sure your payments are completed as agreed to ensure you receive the full benefit.
